**Date:** 2026-05-30  
**For:** Local ISP owner considering franchise vs equity investment  
**Ask:** ₽1.5M for equity stake (not a franchise fee)  
**Tone:** Calm, concrete, no hype. No direct competitor bashing.  
**Format:** PDF deck (10–12 slides) + 2–3 page FAQ doc.

---

## 1. Context & Strategy

Investor owns a local ISP and was looking at buying a franchise (AI-agency / automation services).  
We are offering something different: **ownership of a technology asset** that already has live deployments, paying customers, and a working product.

Key message:  
> "Franchise = you buy the right to work under someone else's brand.  
> Here you own a piece of the platform, the code, the customer base, and the team."

**ISP angle:** His existing subscriber base is an immediate testbed and distribution channel. AI support agent for an ISP is a concrete, measurable use-case with clear ROI (reduce support headcount, cut response time, handle payment/tariff questions 24/7).

## 3. FAQ Document (2–3 Pages)

### Q: Why not just buy a franchise? It's cheaper upfront.
**A:** Franchise gives you a playbook and a brand. You still pay royalties every month, and you don't own anything. Here you own a percentage of the code, the customer base, and future revenue. If the company sells in 3 years, you get [INVESTOR_SHARE]% of the sale. With a franchise, you get nothing.

### Q: What if the business doesn't grow?
**A:** The product already generates revenue. Worst-case scenario: the asset (code + clients) still has value and can be sold or pivoted. We keep 2 months of reserve. You have full visibility into burn rate and can call a shareholder vote on strategy changes. Plus, your own ISP saves money from Month 1 via the support bot.

### Q: How much time do I need to spend on this?
**A:** 5–10 hours/week initially — mostly introductions to other ISP owners and feedback on bot performance. The dedicated developer handles technical execution. After Month 6, if verticals are working, it can be passive.

### Q: How do I get my money back?
**A:** Three paths: (1) annual dividends once profitable, (2) selling your stake to a new investor at a higher valuation, (3) company buyback after 24 months. All terms in the shareholder agreement.

### Q: Why not a loan instead of equity?
**A:** A loan means fixed payments regardless of performance. Equity means we win or lose together. It also gives you governance rights and upside — a lender gets none of that.

### Q: Who owns the code?
**A:** The OOO owns all IP. As a shareholder, you own a piece of that IP. Founders cannot sell the code without shareholder approval.

### Q: What happens if founders leave?
**A:** 3-year vesting means unvested shares return to the company. The code and client base remain with the OOO. We hire replacement talent from the reserve budget.

### Q: Can I use this for my own ISP?
**A:** Yes. White-label deployment for your ISP is included and is the Month 1 priority. You get the tech; we handle maintenance. Your subscribers never know it's "My Agent" — it answers under your brand.

### Q: Will this actually work for ISP support?
**A:** Yes. The platform already handles structured Q&A, scheduled workflows, and Telegram integration. ISP support is 80% repetitive (tariff info, outage status, payment confirmation) — perfect for AI. We pilot on your real data before selling to anyone else.

## 5. Demo Script (For Live Meeting)

**5 minutes. Investor's phone or laptop.**

1. Open `localhost:8020/` (or production URL)
2. Type: *"AI support consultant for a local internet provider"*
3. Show generated agent: name, role, skills (tariff explanation, outage reports, payment help)
4. Ask follow-up: *"My internet is slow, what do I do?"* → live reply with troubleshooting steps
5. Ask: *"How much is the 100 Mbps tariff?"* → reply with pricing and upgrade options
6. Switch to `/showcase` → show how other verticals work
7. Open `/app/chat` → show multi-thread chat with real agent handling tool calls
8. Close: "This is not a mockup. This is what we deploy for your subscribers next month."
